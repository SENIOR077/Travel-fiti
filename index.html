About Us

Our Story

Company Background

Founded in the heart of East Africa, TRAVELFITY was born out of a passion for adventure and regional connectivity. We saw an opportunity to build a travel platform that brings together East African cities like Nairobi, Arusha, Kampala, Mombasa, and Kigali under one seamless booking experience. Our journey started with a small fleet and a big dream — to make cross-border bus travel comfortable, reliable, and inspiring.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To connect East African cities and people through safe, affordable, and unforgettable bus journeys.

Vision: To be the leading regional travel companion across East Africa — where every road leads to new discovery.

Why We Travel

Travel isn't just about the destination — it's about the story you write along the way. At TRAVELFITY, we believe that bus travel allows people to explore their culture, meet new people, support local economies, and rediscover the beauty of Africa. We aim to make every ride part of your bigger journey.

A Word from Our Founder

“I started TRAVELFITY because I wanted to change how we move around East Africa. As someone who’s traveled the region by road, I know the value of comfort, safety, and good planning. TRAVELFITY is built for travelers like me — curious, bold, and connected to the road.”
— Dennis Kemboi, Founder & Creative Lead
